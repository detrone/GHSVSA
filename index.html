Vietnamese Student Association

Garfield High School

VSA is a club that aims to bring together students to learn about our history and honor our culture. Although we hope to build a community of students with Vietnamese descent, we welcome anyone interested in our culture to join us!

We have weekly meetings in Ms. Amador's room. We hangout and talk... and occasionally have snacks and coffee!
